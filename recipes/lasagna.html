Lasagna

Lasagna is a pasta product, a thin sheet of dough in the shape of a square or rectangle, and also a dish of Italian cuisine, traditionally prepared from thin sheets of dough with layers of various fillings.

Ingredients (for 6 servings)

  • Lasagna sheets - 250 g
  • Ground meat (beef) - 450 g
  • Onion - 1
  • Carrot - 1
  • Tomato sauce - 60 g
  • Tomato paste - 60 g
  • Butter - 60 g + 30 g
  • Flour - 60 g
  • Milk - 800 ml
  • Parmesan cheese - 120 g
  • Nutmeg - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 2 tsp
  • Ground pepper - 1 tsp
  1. Prepare the béchamel sauce. Melt 60g of butter in a deep frying pan, add the flour, and, stirring vigorously, gradually pour in the hot milk. Season with salt and nutmeg, bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and remove from heat.
  2. Prepare the Bolognese sauce. Finely chop the onion and grate the carrots.
  3. In a deep frying pan, fry the onion in butter until half-cooked. Add the carrots and fry for about 5 minutes.
  4. Then add the minced meat, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and mix well. Fry over low heat for about 10 more minutes.
  5. Next, add the tomato sauce and tomato paste. Stir and cook the Bolognese sauce for another 10 minutes.
  6. Spread some béchamel sauce on the bottom of the pan. Place a lasagna sheet on top (be sure to follow the instructions on the prepared sheets). Then spread some Bolognese sauce. Pour some béchamel sauce on top and sprinkle with grated Parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  8. Drizzle the top layer of lasagna with béchamel sauce and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake the lasagna in the oven for about 40 minutes at 180-190 degrees Celsius, until golden brown.
  9. Remove the cooked lasagna from the oven, let it rest for 10 minutes, and then cut into portions. Decorate and enjoy!
